About The Hike


Mt. Manalmon and Mt. Gola

Part of the historical Biak-na-Bato National Park is an outdoor destination called Mt. Manalmon, in San Miguel, Bulacan. Although merely a hill, strictly speaking, Mt. Manalmon possesses novel landscapes, including those of Madlum River, whose rock formations compensate for an otherwise unremarkable environ (Henares, 2007).

  • 7:15 AM - ETA Mt. Manalmon Summit. Quick Snack/Breakfast

Talisayin Cove Camping
Zambales

Talisayin Cove is one of many coves that lay on the coast of Zambales. Apparently its one of the least visited as well given the fact that it has only recently been open to the public. The fact that its not as popular as the other coves pretty much ensures that you'll most likely get the entire beach to yourselves. It doesn't offer much like waterfalls but it does give you the right kind of silence.

About the mountain

Easily accessible from Metro Manila, Rodriguez, Rizal offers several mountains that can be taken on even by the unexperienced. Mts. Hapunang Banoi and Pamitinan are two of the well known mountains in the area. Respectively, reaching over 500 meters above sea level and 400 meters above sea level, both are considered as minor climb and can take 2-3 hours each. The mountains are situated near the town of Brgy Wawa where the famous manmade dam of Wawa is located as well as a cave for exploring. If set on an early climb, chances are one can view the sea of clouds.
